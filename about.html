Our Story

From Lola's Kitchen to Your Table

Camalaniugan FoodHub was born in 2018 from a simple dream: to share the rich, authentic flavors of Cagayan's native delicacies with our community. What started as weekend kakanin-making sessions in a small home kitchen quickly grew into something much bigger.

Our founder, Fe Castro, learned these time-honored recipes from her grandmother — a master of traditional Filipino cooking whose bibingka and biko were legendary at every fiesta and family gathering in Camalaniugan. Every product we make today carries that legacy.

We use only locally sourced ingredients — fresh coconut from nearby farms, premium glutinous rice, and organic palm sugar — to ensure every bite is as nourishing as it is delicious.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To preserve and celebrate the traditional culinary heritage of Camalaniugan, Cagayan by crafting authentic Filipino delicacies using time-honored recipes and locally sourced ingredients — making them accessible to every family through modern e-commerce.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and beloved source of authentic Filipino kakanin in Cagayan Valley — growing from a home kitchen to a thriving local food enterprise that uplifts local farmers, supports the community, and proudly carries Camalaniugan's flavor to the world.
