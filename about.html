Our Story

Connecting Restaurants,
Empowering Communities

Camalaniugan FoodHub was launched in 2026 as a digital marketplace designed to connect local restaurants with customers through a seamless online ordering experience. We saw an opportunity to bring the rich food culture of Camalaniugan and Cagayan Valley into the digital age.

What began as a small initiative to help neighborhood eateries reach more customers has grown into a thriving platform with 8 partner restaurants — from Juval's Grill and Alfresco Pizzeria to Korean N Kanto Eats, Big Brew, and Juana's Food Hub. Each one offers their best-selling dishes through our site.

Beyond ordering, we also serve as a Profit Development Program — equipping local food entrepreneurs with training, digital tools, and self-assessment resources to grow their businesses sustainably.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower local food businesses in Camalaniugan by providing a modern, accessible e-commerce platform that connects partner restaurants with customers — making it easy to discover, order, and enjoy the best local cuisine while supporting the community's economic growth.

Our Vision

To become the leading local food hub platform in Cagayan Valley — a digital ecosystem where every restaurant in the region can thrive online, every customer can conveniently access authentic local cuisine, and every food entrepreneur has the tools and knowledge to grow their business profitably.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Local First

We exclusively partner with restaurants and food businesses rooted in Camalaniugan and nearby towns — keeping revenue within the community.

Quality Assured

Every partner restaurant is verified for food quality, hygiene standards, and consistent service — so customers can order with confidence.

Community Growth

We provide training, digital tools, and profit development programs to help local food entrepreneurs grow their businesses sustainably.

Seamless Experience

From browsing menus to checkout and delivery — our platform makes ordering from multiple restaurants fast, easy, and reliable.
