Our Story

From Our Kitchen,
With Love & Tradition

Camalaniugan FoodHub started as a small home-based kitchen in 2026, sharing our lola's kakanin recipes with neighbors and friends. What began as a passion project quickly became the community's favorite source for authentic Filipino delicacies.

Every product is made by hand, using locally sourced coconut, rice, and sugar — honoring the flavors that define Cagayan's rich culinary heritage.
